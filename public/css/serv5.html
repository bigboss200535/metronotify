Ultrasound Scan Services

OAK offers various ultrasound services that aim to give prospective parents a chance to see their bundle of joy before birth.

We supply a full suite of on-demand services, including a wide range of ultrasound options.

Our ultrasound services include 3D and 4D scans. This supplies a detailed evaluation of your baby’s organs, offering information on the baby's wellbeing.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Today is my first visit to OAK. Where do I start?
Kindly speak to our front desk personnel and inform them of your status as a first timer. Your basic data will be taken into our system and your preferred mode of payment would be recorded; including cash or credit based on insurance/employer coverage. The front desk personnel will inform you of your next step.
2. I am a foreigner; do I need a special registration?
No, we are all one. Where applicable, submit all the necessary referral forms and insurance documents to aid in the registration processes.
3. Do you take health insurance? Which of them do you accept?
Yes, we accept a wide range of private health insurance claims. Kindly click here for more info.
4. Do you accept card payments and foreign currencies?
Yes, we accept credit/debit cards including VISA, GHLink, and MasterCard. We also accept foreign currencies at an approved exchange rate of the facility.
5. Will you permit visitors while I am on admission?
Yes, we permit guests every day of the week.
6. How can I get a medical report after my visit?
Kindly request from your treating doctor for the required medical report if you are an out-patient. All in-patients are permitted to ask their attending doctor for any type of medical report. Your report may take 1-3 days to be ready.
7. Are all the specialist consultants available 24/7?
Physicians at the Family Medicine department are available 24hours. Other specialists are available at specified days and times within the week Kindly click here for more info.
8. I must visit the doctor again, what do I do?
Kindly speak to the front desk personnel to make a review appointment if you are present at the facility. You may also call or WhatsApp any of our office numbers to book an appointment.
9. Am I permitted to see a specialist without booking an appointment?
Yes, you will be given time to see the specialist on arrival.
10. How is the appointment system at OAK?
Our appointment system is flexible. Please call us on +233 (0) 59 964 2520 or +233 (0) 50 976 0659 for your appointment to be scheduled.
11. I called to book an appointment, where do I go when I arrive at the hospital?
Please speak with our front desk personnel for assistance.
12. I met a doctor and was asked to see another doctor in a different department, what should I do?
Please you will have to inform the front desk personnel. If you are out of the hospital, kindly call any of our office numbers for assistance.
13. How do I get my lab investigations done?
The laboratory scientist on duty will check our system and take appropriate sample. The result will be sent to the requesting doctor.
14. Will I get a copy of my laboratory reports?
The result will be communicated electronically to the requesting doctor. However, if you need a hardcopy for personal reasons, you can pass by the lab after meeting with your requesting doctor.
15. Do you take part-payment for your services?
We usually advice that an advance payment be made at the time of admission. However, if full payment cannot be done, you are permitted to make part-payment and have a payment plan with the account department before discharge.
16. Will you permit a relative to stay with me when admitted at OAK?
We have our professional caregivers, nurses and midwives who are always ready to assist patients on admission. However, relatives are permitted to stay with the patient if they want.
17. I want to visit a friend on admission at OAK, where do I go?
Kindly speak to our front desk personnel, you will answer few questions and you would be directed to the nurses’ station for further assistance.
18. I have a prescription from a different hospital, can I buy the drugs from your pharmacy?
Yes, kindly visit our pharmacy any time of the day within the week.
19. The doctor prescribed a drug for me, I missed a dose, what do I do?
You may need to speak with our pharmacists, so contact us on +233 (0) 59 964 2520 or +233 (0) 50 976 0659 for assistance.

We deliver the needed healthcare services across all capabilities. We look forward to your visit. We are readily available for further discussions.
